China‘s TV production quality has been increased dramatically in the last couple years. Many new shows continue their success in 2022. We have selected 10 different reality shows from various broadcasters to give you an overview of entertaining reality shows.

1. Wonderland(五十公里桃花坞2)

"Fifty Kilometers of Taohuawu 2" is a social observation reality show about life. This season, 15 guests are invited to Taohuawu, a seaside beach 50 kilometers away from the city center, to live together for 21 days. The program will explore the various aspects of group life in contemporary society under the changes of different social distances, and write new answers to a better group life.

Each episode is based on a classic IP to restore, adapt, and "travel" the real scene, and perform hilarious interpretations of the original drama; "Cute Detective Family" through Plot doubts and fun games, get clues to the case, and find the truth happily. The second season also added a film and television case presentation, not just a game, but more like a movie. The program aims to create an immersive experience of reasoning and detectives. While paying tribute to the classics, it also conveys the positive emotional guidance of emo goodbye and cute exploration to the society.
