Many new love and romance shows are released in China this year. We look at the 8 biggest dating shows produced by big brands such as Tencent, Bilibili, Mango TV, iQiyi and Youku.

1. Go for Spring(春日迟迟再出发)

Eight single men and women who have not yet healed from the pain caused by their previous failed marriages embarked on a "limited trip" for four consecutive weekends. They accompany each other and heal each other in the mountains, forests and seas. They also gradually open their hearts in the midweek misses and weekend gatherings, regaining their hearts and the courage to love again.

The 5 guests have different social value collisions from different angles by observing and reasoning about the development trend of the ambiguous relationship among amateurs.

3. So In Love(没谈过恋爱的我)

As the first first love growth reality show in China, the show is the first attempt to turn the camera to groups without love experience. On the road of chasing love, everyone used to be "I who have never been in love". In order to record this common stage that everyone will go through, the program invites several single young people who have never been in love to share their living conditions and emotions. The growth is presented one by one, and the audience can feel the growth process of first love together.

4. Yes, I Do Season 3(喜欢你我也是 第三季)

The third season of "Like You and Me Too" will invite nine single men and women who yearn for true love to break away from the shackles of work and life, escape from the hustle and bustle of the city, and board a romantic island of true love together to spend a 21-day true love holiday , and finally made the choice of "I like you and me too". The program builds True Love Island into a utopia exclusively for lovers. Under the unique rules of communication on the island, learn to fall in love, accompany each other, grow together, and trigger each other's sincerity layer by layer from the outside to the inside. Can she (he) successfully find her true love power and feel the most beautiful romantic moment under the blue sea and blue sky, between the bonfire and three meals, among the waves and singing?

6. Hello summer(相遇的夏天)

The program is a summer travel social reality show. 9 amateur guests will live in a seaside courtyard together and become each other's neighbors, and spend a 14-day "adults' small vacation" together. During this period, they will use different dating methods to feel nature, perceive themselves, and explore social relationship between people. The small courtyard mode of the show not only divides the social distance for them, but also provides a unique social space at the same time, showing a very novel social mode to the current young people, and can better show their true self during the holiday , Use the most authentic side to explore the true meaning of love. The encounter observation group composed of Jiang Zhenyu, Liu Yan, Ouyang Nana, Wang Cong, Yang Di, and Zeng Shunxi will observe the social process of amateur guests from the perspective of different age groups, and output their own attitudes about emotions. Mature views and ideas of young minds collide with sparks of opinion.

7. Love Discovery Club(一起探恋爱)

This is the first vegan youth dating program. The program is attended by 8 single men and women. During 15 days, they have to participate in many different types of "script experiences" and colorful daily dates. The program abandons the traditional model of variety show lovers sitting in the studio to comment on amateur guests, and advocates that love does not need to be evaluated, and there is no need to know right or wrong. It allows young people to interweave and ferment independently in the virtual world created by in-depth social scenes and the real dating world of daily life scenes, and heat up layer by layer. The program focuses on guiding the positive view of love. The interactive activities of the amateur guests throughout the season will be carried out around love-related keywords such as "trust, acceptance, tolerance, mutual assistance, dedication, sacrifice" and other keywords, and jointly construct a meeting that fits the demands of young people today. Positive outlook on love and emotion, providing guidance and advice for young people to make friends.

8. The Late Lovers(迟到的恋人)

A humanities documentary-oriented social observation reality show. Eight single men and women with a history of marriage or quasi-marriage regained the courage to remarry and fall in love during the two journeys they traveled together. When sensitive marriage and love issues such as remarriage and child custody are brought to the table, how can they resolve them decently in an adult way? At the same time, artists with different experiences in the observation room will convey the correct views on marriage and love to young people through the discussion of prominent marriage and love issues in society reflected in them, and encourage all kinds of people to bravely pursue love.
