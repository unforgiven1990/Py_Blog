Douban is a Chinese rating website for TV shows and movie, similar to the imdb.com. We have ranked15 highest rated reality TV shows from 2022 in descending order. Please note that this order is given by the Chinese audience. Some reasons for the high rating are not nesseary the production quality, but the "pureness" and "honesty" of the series which shows the most real side of the stars without adding fake emotions and stories. Chinese audiences have seen too much scripted content in the past and some people are evaluating truly unscripted stories more than ever.

"Happy Start Again" is a music travel reality show jointly produced by Mango TV and Daqian Films. In the show, the six brothers Chen Chusheng, Lu Hu, Su Xing, Wang Lixin, Wang Zhengliang, and Zhang Yuan gathered again to open a new era together. journey. During their journey, they experienced survival on a deserted island, experienced camping by the sea, and also worked part-time on the crew to earn box lunches... There were surprises, laughter, and even more touching. The program group aimed at the fast men's planning for their own group, combined with the actual budget "downgrade" processing, "collected" a trip to realize their dreams. While walking and singing as a fixed project, the fast boys will independently design this 15-year-old group show. With a high degree of freedom, how to arrange themselves clearly, this is destined to be a hilarious journey full of outrageous and unexpected trip.
