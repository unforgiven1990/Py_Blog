While some old formats like Happy Camp (快乐大本营) discontinued in 2022, some other formats are still produced. We have selected 16 TV reality formats from China that are still updated in 2022. All of these formats are at least in their 3rd generation. While some formats (such as Sing!China aka The voice of China) are international formats, other franchises are Chinese local. Check out this list to see what people in China are watching.

1. Running Man Season 10(奔跑吧 第十季)

"Let's Run Season Six" is a sports variety show launched by Zhejiang Satellite TV. It is a similar show to the Korean format "Running Man" where celebrities compete together in various joyful minigames. The franchise has already produced the 10th season in China and it is most likely to continue. It is perfect for audiences who wants to see some high energy actions.
